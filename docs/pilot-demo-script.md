# Pilot Demo Script

## Demo Goal

Show leadership that this is not an AI chatbot demo. It is a capability-risk workflow for industrial L&D.

## Opening

"Today L&D can often prove training attendance, but leadership needs to know where workforce capability risk exists and what action will reduce it. SkillPath AI turns fragmented learning and operational signals into worker guidance, supervisor coaching, and leadership visibility."

## Step 1: Leadership Command Center

Show:

- capability risk
- training precision
- knowledge risk

Say:

"This gives leadership a risk lens, not just a learning activity report."

## Step 2: Select Role And Worker

Choose:

- CNC Operator
- Asha R.
- Night shift

Say:

"The system starts with the role, worker context, and operational exposure. The goal is personalized action without making the worker search through long documents."

## Step 3: Worker Coach

Use sample question:

"Why do dimensional defects keep happening after tool change?"

Show:

- source
- confidence
- short answer
- learning recommendation
- escalation trigger

Say:

"The worker gets a simple answer and safe next action. The AI does not replace process sign-off."

## Step 4: Supervisor Cockpit

Generate coaching plan.

Say:

"The supervisor gets a two-week coaching plan tied to evidence. This closes the loop between learning and actual performance."

## Step 5: Knowledge Capture

Generate capture pack.

Say:

"This is the secondary opportunity: expert know-how becomes reusable training content before it leaves the organization."

## Step 6: Business Case

Use:

- 120 pilot workers
- 2 repeated questions per worker per week
- 5 minutes saved per resolved question

Say:

"This conservative estimate counts only supervisor time saved. The larger upside is avoided rework, faster onboarding, reduced repeat errors, and lower expert dependency."

## Closing Ask

"Approve an 8-12 week pilot in one plant, focused on three critical roles and one operating metric. The funding decision should depend on whether capability risk is important enough to manage continuously."
