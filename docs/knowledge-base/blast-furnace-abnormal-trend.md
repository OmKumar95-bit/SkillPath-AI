# Blast Furnace Abnormal Trend Guidance

Approved source: Tata Steel Jamshedpur training sample for L&D pilot.

For a blast furnace operator, abnormal temperature movement should be treated as an early risk signal. The operator should check recent burden pattern, hot blast parameters, gas trend, cooling water alarms, pressure movement, and the previous shift log.

The worker should not change operating assumptions casually. When temperature trend is abnormal with gas, pressure, or cooling-water warning signs, the operator should escalate to the shift in-charge immediately.

Training use: abnormal furnace trend recognition, burden distribution awareness, and safe escalation.
