# Steel Plant Expert Knowledge Capture

Approved source: Tata Steel Jamshedpur training sample for L&D pilot.

Experienced steel plant operators and technicians often hold practical knowledge about early warning signs, unsafe shortcuts, recurring breakdown patterns, quality judgement, and escalation timing.

Supervisors should interview experts using structured prompts: symptoms, early signs, common mistakes, safe check sequence, root cause clues, escalation trigger, and short coaching scenario.

Training use: tacit knowledge capture, retiring workforce knowledge retention, supervisor coaching, and reusable micro-learning.
