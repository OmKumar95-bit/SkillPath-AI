# Steel Melting Shop Ladle Temperature Control

Approved source: Tata Steel Jamshedpur training sample for L&D pilot.

When ladle temperature is outside the target range, the technician should confirm the target grade, sample timing, ladle condition, waiting time, process instruction, and pending lab confirmation before release.

Temperature or chemistry correction should happen only through authorized process guidance. For molten metal handling, PPE, exclusion zone discipline, communication, and shift in-charge approval are mandatory.

Training use: ladle temperature control, heat release decision, molten metal safety, and shift handover clarity.
