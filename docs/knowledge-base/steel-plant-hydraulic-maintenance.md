# Steel Plant Hydraulic Maintenance Guidance

Approved source: Tata Steel Jamshedpur training sample for L&D pilot.

Before replacing hydraulic components in steel plant equipment, the maintenance technician should check pressure drop, oil temperature, filter condition, actuator leakage, recent maintenance history, and operating context.

Do not bypass interlocks, remove guards, ignore lockout requirements, or use unsafe shortcuts during pressure testing. Complex isolation must be supervised and signed off.

Training use: hydraulic fault diagnosis, LOTO and energy isolation, preventive maintenance planning, and breakdown knowledge documentation.
