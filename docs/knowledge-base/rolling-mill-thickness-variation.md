# Rolling Mill Thickness Variation Guidance

Approved source: Tata Steel Jamshedpur training sample for L&D pilot.

When strip thickness variation increases, the rolling mill operator should check roll gap setting, gauge feedback, speed changes, incoming material condition, mill stand condition, and recent maintenance notes.

The operator should separate measurement issue from process issue before repeated adjustment. If variation continues after verified setting and measurement checks, escalate to the rolling mill supervisor or quality engineer.

Training use: thickness control troubleshooting, coil defect classification, line stop protocol, and customer quality protection.
